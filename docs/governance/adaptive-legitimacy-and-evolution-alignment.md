# Adaptive Legitimacy and Evolution Alignment

Status: Evolution restraint baseline.

## Purpose

Adaptive legitimacy ensures the platform can evolve without losing civic purpose, public trust, governance restraint, privacy boundaries, human accountability, or anti-surveillance identity.

## Mission Drift Signals

- Expansion beyond original civic purpose.
- Silent feature creep.
- Authority scope growth.
- Governance dilution.
- Operational overreach.
- Infrastructure identity erosion.

## Legitimacy Signals

- Public expectation shifts.
- Civic norm changes.
- Institutional trust shifts.
- Generational perception gaps.
- Regional legitimacy variance.
- Long-term reputation volatility.

## Technology Restraint Risks

- AI over-automation.
- Predictive governance incentives.
- Excessive telemetry dependence.
- Biometric expansion pressure.
- Automated authority escalation.
- Optimization-driven surveillance drift.

## Principle Preservation

The platform must preserve:

- Minimum necessary authority.
- Reversible deployment.
- Uncertainty visibility.
- Human accountability.
- Operational restraint.
- Privacy boundaries.
- Explainable governance.

## Societal Alignment Checks

The platform must periodically verify:

- Communities still perceive value.
- Infrastructure behavior matches lived reality.
- Governance remains understandable.
- Operators remain trusted.
- Institutional participation remains healthy.

## Evolutionary Oversight

Required renewal mechanisms:

- Periodic legitimacy reviews.
- External civic review pathways.
- Governance renewal checkpoints.
- Institutional drift audits.
- Principle compliance reviews.
- Infrastructure simplification reviews.

## Anti-Permanent-Emergency Protection

The platform must detect:

- Normalization of exceptional powers.
- Endless degraded-state governance.
- Perpetual emergency authority.
- Escalation habituation.
- Chronic operator override culture.

## Decision States

- `adaptive-legitimacy-freeze`: evolution is unsafe because identity, principles, legitimacy, oversight, or emergency boundaries are failing.
- `restrict-evolution-and-renew-legitimacy`: evolution must pause for civic renewal and governance review.
- `heightened-legitimacy-watch`: evolution may continue only with increased review.
- `continue-principled-evolution`: restrained evolution may continue without increasing authority.

## Non-Certifying Boundary

Adaptive legitimacy guides evolution. It does not certify readiness, authorize expansion, or override failed survivability, stress, convergence, integrity, or sustainability gates.
