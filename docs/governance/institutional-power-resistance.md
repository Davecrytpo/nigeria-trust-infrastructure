# Institutional Power Resistance and Civic Integrity

Status: Civic integrity control baseline.

## Purpose

Institutional power resistance ensures the platform remains civic coordination infrastructure rather than becoming centralized surveillance, coercive authority, or opaque institutional control.

## Power Concentration Signals

- Authority accumulation.
- Hidden operational override expansion.
- Supervisor overreach.
- Governance bypass attempts.
- Centralized approval dependency.
- Institutional control concentration.

## Surveillance Drift Signals

- Unauthorized data retention.
- Operational scope creep.
- Excessive telemetry expansion.
- Hidden tracking incentives.
- Non-expiring location storage.
- Identity over-collection.
- Cross-incident profiling pressure.

## Governance Bypass Signals

- Undocumented override actions.
- Emergency-power abuse.
- Operator shortcut escalation.
- Hidden administrative authority.
- Silent policy modification.
- Unreviewed supervisor intervention.

## Political Pressure Signals

- Politically motivated escalation pressure.
- Selective incident prioritization attempts.
- Institutional favoritism.
- Suppression incentives.
- Reputational manipulation requests.
- Narrative-control pressure.

## Rights Preservation Controls

- Minimum necessary data.
- Reversible authority.
- Auditable governance.
- Explainable intervention.
- Constrained automation.
- Independent reviewability.
- Resident privacy boundaries.
- Responder consent boundaries.
- Sensitive telemetry expiration.
- Constrained identity linkage.

## Trusted Oversight Requirements

- Multi-party review.
- Distributed governance visibility.
- Supervisor accountability chains.
- Independent audit pathways.
- Conflict-of-interest disclosure.
- Override transparency.

## Decision States

- `civic-integrity-freeze`: power, surveillance, bypass, political pressure, rights, or oversight failures require immediate freeze.
- `restrict-authority-and-require-independent-review`: authority must be constrained and reviewed independently.
- `heightened-civic-integrity-watch`: risk is visible and requires increased monitoring.
- `continue-rights-preserving-supervision`: distributed oversight and privacy boundaries are intact.

## Non-Certifying Boundary

Civic integrity controls restrict power. They do not certify readiness, authorize expansion, or override survivability failures.
