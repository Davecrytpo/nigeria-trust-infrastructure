# Operational Liability Model

Status: Initial accountability baseline.

## Liability Domains

- Responder misconduct.
- Operator negligence.
- Delayed coordination harm.
- Institutional non-response.
- Telecom-induced failure.
- Escalation timing dispute.
- Evidence integrity challenge.

## Liability Bands

- Low: routine audit.
- Moderate: incident review and operator note audit.
- High: supervisor review, evidence freeze, and corrective action plan.
- Severe: external review, evidence freeze, deployment freeze, and legal export package.

## Evidence Rules

- Do not overwrite disputed timelines.
- Preserve telecom receipt uncertainty.
- Preserve operator decision context.
- Preserve responder assignment and acknowledgment records.
- Preserve institutional contact attempts.
- Every legal export must include export actor, reason, timestamp, scope, and recipient.
