# Pilot Governance Board

Status: Initial board structure.

## Board Functions

- Approve or deny pilot activation.
- Approve limited-hours or shadow-mode operation.
- Pause or freeze the pilot.
- Review serious incidents and trust anomalies.
- Review responder conduct.
- Review telecom reliability and operational evidence.
- Approve expansion only after readiness, stress, convergence, adaptive, maturity, governance, and pilot gates support it.

## Required Members

- Operations supervisor.
- Trust administrator.
- Field coordinator.
- Institutional liaison.
- Audit reviewer.
- Community representative or designated civic observer.

## Standing Agenda

- Field incident review.
- Readiness and stress findings.
- Convergence and adaptive recovery findings.
- Telecom reliability evidence.
- Trust durability and responder reliability.
- Civic trust complaints and appeals.
- Audit backlog and evidence integrity.
- Go, pause, freeze, or remediation decision.
