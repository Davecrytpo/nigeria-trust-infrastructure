# Yaba Safe Pilot Governance

Status: Controlled pilot governance baseline.

## Pilot Roles

- Pilot review board: approves activation, limitation, freeze, and post-incident review.
- Operations supervisor: owns live escalation quality and degraded-mode rollback.
- Trust administrator: owns responder verification, suspension, reinstatement, and trust anomaly review.
- Field coordinator: owns onboarding, community communication, field attendance, and local feedback.
- Institutional liaison: owns clinic, police, estate, school, and partner coordination paths.
- Audit reviewer: owns evidence access review and governance compliance.

## Operating Modes

- Controlled operational pilot: all governance controls are satisfied.
- Limited supervised pilot: constrained hours, active supervisor coverage, and explicit incident limits.
- Dry runs only: no public live coordination; exercises and shadow validation only.
- Do not run: governance controls are unsafe.

## Review Cadence

- Daily shift review during live pilot windows.
- Weekly responder reliability review.
- Weekly telecom and queue recovery review.
- Biweekly governance and audit review.
- Immediate review for serious harm, misconduct, evidence dispute, or public trust complaint.
