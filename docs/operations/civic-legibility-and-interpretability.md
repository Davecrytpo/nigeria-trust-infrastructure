# Civic Legibility and Interpretability

Status: Explanation and accountability baseline.

## Purpose

Civic legibility ensures operators, supervisors, institutions, responders, and residents can understand why the platform recommends restrictions, escalations, degraded modes, shutdowns, rollbacks, or refusals.

## Explanation Principles

- Use plain language.
- State uncertainty directly.
- Avoid false precision.
- Explain active constraints.
- Explain what evidence is missing.
- Explain why human supervision remains required.
- Never present a decision explanation as readiness certification.

## Decisions Requiring Explanation

- Escalation decisions.
- Degraded-mode activation.
- Trust restrictions.
- Responder prioritization.
- Shutdown recommendations.
- Uncertainty freezes.
- Rollback decisions.
- Certification withholding.

## Uncertainty Visibility

Explanations must surface:

- Incomplete information.
- Unstable confidence.
- Contradictory observations.
- Telecom uncertainty.
- Operational ambiguity.
- Active degraded state limitations.
- Unresolved incident evidence.

## Governance Interpretability

Supervisors and institutions must be able to see:

- Why restrictions were applied.
- Why certification was withheld.
- Why expansion was frozen.
- Which review path applies.
- Which audit record is required.

## Community Trust Legibility

Resident-facing explanations must state:

- What data is collected.
- What data is not collected.
- Why privacy boundaries exist.
- Why shutdown authority exists.
- Why human supervision remains dominant.
- Why the platform avoids full automation.

## Accountability Trace

Major operational actions require:

- Approver.
- Reason.
- Uncertainty snapshot.
- Active constraints.
- Decision assumptions.
- Timestamp.

Actions with incomplete accountability traces must not be finalized.

## Anti-Surveillance Boundary

The platform must explicitly reinforce:

- No facial recognition.
- No continuous tracking.
- No biometric identity collection.
- No hidden authority expansion.
- No automated policing.
- Minimal, purpose-bound, auditable, time-limited data.

## Non-Certifying Boundary

Legibility explains decisions. It does not certify readiness, override failed survivability gates, or convert uncertainty into certainty.
