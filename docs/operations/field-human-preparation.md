# Field Human Preparation

Status: Initial field readiness baseline.

## Required Preparation

- Responder onboarding and certification.
- Operator field training.
- Supervisor review training.
- Escalation communication drills.
- Degraded-mode operational training.
- Telecom failure handling drills.
- Incident review workflow training.

## Training Principles

- Responders must know what they are not authorized to do.
- Operators must practice uncertainty, degraded modes, and overload.
- Supervisors must practice rollback, freeze, and escalation authority.
- Every participant must understand that the pilot is a controlled validation exercise, not a public emergency service guarantee.

## Minimum Drill Set

- Weak telecom dispatch drill.
- Delayed SMS and duplicate receipt drill.
- Replay divergence and manual review drill.
- Operator overload and supervisor handoff drill.
- Trust anomaly and responder freeze drill.
- Institutional non-response drill.
- Emergency shutdown drill.
