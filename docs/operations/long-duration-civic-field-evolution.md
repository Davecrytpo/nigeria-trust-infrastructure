# Long-Duration Civic Field Evolution

Status: Slow-failure detection baseline.

## Purpose

Long-duration civic field evolution studies how restricted live operations degrade over time. Prolonged operation is not proof of safety. It may reveal slow infrastructure decay that short tests cannot expose.

## Drift Domains

- Responder reliability decline.
- Operator judgment drift.
- Governance inconsistency growth.
- Telecom degradation recurrence.
- Institutional participation volatility.
- Audit quality degradation.
- Escalation timing deterioration.

## Chronic Normalization Risks

- Operators becoming comfortable with degraded modes.
- Responders treating instability as normal.
- Governance tolerance inflation.
- Increasing acceptance of delayed coordination.
- Silent reduction in operational standards.

## Human Fatigue Evolution

- Repeated alert exhaustion.
- Supervisory burnout.
- Cognitive overload accumulation.
- Escalation avoidance.
- Trust-review fatigue.
- Risk-signal desensitization.

## Telecom Evolution

- Provider reliability shifts.
- Recurring outage geography.
- Seasonal congestion.
- SMS delivery drift.
- Android fragmentation failure growth.
- Device battery degradation.

## Governance and Trust Durability

- Institutional participation persistence.
- Supervisor consistency.
- Audit completion reliability.
- Policy adherence decay.
- Emergency authority misuse risk.
- Governance shortcut emergence.
- Resident trust decline.
- Responder abandonment growth.
- Coordination disappointment accumulation.

## Continuity Risks

- Staffing continuity gaps.
- Supervisor succession weakness.
- Supervisor replacement impact.
- Single-institution dependency.
- Localized knowledge concentration.

## Decision States

- `freeze-prolonged-live-operation`: chronic normalization or governance failure requires immediate pause.
- `restrict-and-reset-long-duration-operations`: slow decay requires operational reset and stricter supervision.
- `supervised-continuity-watch`: early drift requires continuity monitoring.
- `continue-limited-long-duration-learning`: limited learning may continue without increasing authority.

## Non-Certifying Boundary

Long-duration operation cannot certify readiness. The system must not interpret endurance alone as safety, especially while survivability, stress, or convergence gates remain failed.
