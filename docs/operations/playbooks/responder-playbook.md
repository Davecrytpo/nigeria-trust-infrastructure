# Responder Playbook

Status: Controlled pilot baseline.

## Responder Duties

Responders provide verified local assistance within their approved territory, skill category, and availability window. The platform must never assume a responder is police, medical staff, or emergency authority unless their institutional role is verified.

## Response Flow

1. Acknowledge or reject the incident quickly.
2. Share realistic ETA if accepting.
3. Move only when personally safe.
4. Update arrival, unable-to-locate, handoff, and completion states.
5. Escalate to operator if the incident is dangerous, unclear, outside training, or beyond capacity.

## Safety Rules

- Do not enter violent, fire, flood, medical, or criminal situations beyond training.
- Do not transport injured people unless cleared by protocol or institutional partner.
- Do not request private evidence from residents outside incident need.
- Do not share incident details on public chats or social media.
- Do not continue responding after suspension, fatigue, or safety compromise.

## Accountability

Every acceptance, rejection, location update, status change, and handoff is auditable. Misconduct, impersonation, abandonment, or evidence tampering triggers trust review and possible removal.
