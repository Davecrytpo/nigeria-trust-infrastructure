# Mission and Problem

Status: Aligned with Master Architecture & Operational Blueprint.

## Core Vision

To build a **digital coordination layer for society** that functions as a large-scale societal coordination infrastructure. The platform aims to reduce confusion, delayed response, lack of trust, poor coordination, location problems, and fragmented communication.

The long-term vision is to establish five foundational pillars:
- Trust Infrastructure
- Emergency Infrastructure
- Community Infrastructure
- Address Infrastructure
- Urban Coordination Infrastructure

## Core Problem

The system addresses the fragmented nature of emergency response in Nigeria and Africa, where:
- Infrastructure is often missing or unreliable.
- Trust in response systems is low.
- Local safety depends on informal and uncoordinated efforts.
- Digital addressing is imprecise.
- Real-time human coordination is difficult during crises.

## Strategic Approach

This is **not just an application; it is infrastructure.** 

The strategy is to:
1. **Solve one painful problem first:** Focus on the Yaba, Lagos pilot.
2. **Build trust:** Establish verified responder systems.
3. **Grow gradually:** Roll out layer by layer, from district to neighborhood to city.
4. **Strengthen reliability:** Ensure the system is essential because society begins to depend on it.

## Working MVP Promise

For a user in the Yaba pilot zone, the platform must:
- Provide multiple **Emergency Entry Methods** (App, SMS, Silent Panic).
- Capture precise location using **Address Infrastructure**.
- Coordinate **Human Infrastructure** (nearby verified responders, clinics, volunteers).
- Maintain an immutable **Incident Flow** and audit trail.

## Initial Success Targets

- **Reliability:** Low data consumption and high accessibility.
- **Speed:** 30-second alert creation.
- **Coordination:** Effective notification of nearby "Emergency Response Nodes".
- **Trust:** 100% verification rate for Tier 1 and Tier 2 responders.

## Immediate Product Questions

- How do we implement the **Silent Panic System** (gestures, button sequences) on mobile?
- What are the standard **SMS Emergency Codes** for the backend to interpret?
- How do we bootstrap the first **Emergency Response Nodes** (police, clinics) in Yaba?
